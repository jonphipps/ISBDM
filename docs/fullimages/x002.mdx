# Entity-relationship diagram of the manifestation of an issue of a diachronic work

<Figure 
  src="/img/x002.png" 
  alt="Entity-relationship diagram of the manifestation of an issue of a diachronic work"
  caption="Entity-relationship diagram of an issue of the manifestation of a diachronic work."

The diagram shows the issue of a diachronic work that is described as a separate related work. There is a choice to describe the manifestation of the diachronic work as a separate related work, or to include the diachronic work or its manifestation in manifestation statements or notes for the manifestation that is being described. Optional entities, manifestation statements, and notes are indicated by dashes.
