#### Stipulations

<div className="stip">
  <Mandatory />
  
  If one or more creator persons are associated with the manifestation, record at least one occurrence of the element. Use the following order of preference to select the value:
  
  1. Person who is a publisher of the manifestation
  2. Person who is a producer of the manifestation
  3. Person who is a manufacturer of the manifestation

  Record additional occurrences of the element, if it is considered to be useful for users of the metadata.
