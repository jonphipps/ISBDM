### Mandatory elements

<div className="guid">
A manifestation element is either mandatory or optional. A cataloguing agency must record at least one occurrence of a mandatory element. A cataloguing agency may choose not to record any occurrence for an optional element.
</div>

<div className="guid">
The mandatory requirement of an element is satisfied if a cataloguing agency records an occurrence of one of its element sub-types. This is a consequence of the semantic hierarchy of elements.

<div className="seeAlso">
*See also*: <InLink href="/docs/intro/i006.html">Metadata utility and processing</InLink>
</div>
</div>

<div className="guid">
A mandatory element is indicated in ISBDM by the symbol <span className="mandatory px-2" title="Mandatory">&#10045;</span> before the reference information for the element.
</div>

<div className="guid">
The mandatory elements are:

* <InLink href="/docs/attributes/1022.html">has category of carrier</InLink>
* <InLink href="/docs/attributes/1264.html">has category of embodied content</InLink>
* <InLink href="/docs/attributes/1218.html">has media type</InLink>
* <InLink href="/docs/attributes/1262.html">has unitary structure</InLink>
* <InLink href="/docs/relationships/1274.html">has appellation of manifestation</InLink>
</div>

<div className="guid">
An element may be mandatory if a condition is satisified; for example that there is a value that is applicable and available. A conditional mandatory stipulation is indicated in ISBDM by the symbol <span className="mandatory px-2" title="Mandatory">&#10045;</span> before the condition.
</div>

<div className="guid">
The elements that have a conditional mandatory stipulation are:

* <InLink href="/docs/statements/1025.html">has manifestation statement</InLink>
  * <InLink href="/docs/statements/1028.html">has manifestation statement of title and responsibility</InLink>
* <InLink href="/docs/relationships/1012.html">has expression embodied in manifestation</InLink> or <InLink href="/docs/relationships/1220.html">has work embodied in manifestation</InLink>
* <InLink href="/docs/relationships/1005.html">has agent associated with manifestation</InLink>
  * <InLink href="/docs/relationships/1019.html">has creator agent of manifestation</InLink>
    * <InLink href="/docs/relationships/1251.html">has creator collective agent of manifestation</InLink> or <InLink href="/docs/relationships/1246.html">has creator person of manifestation</InLink>
* <InLink href="/docs/relationships/1009.html">has place associated with manifestation</InLink>
  * <InLink href="/docs/relationships/1236.html">has place of creation of manifestation</InLink>
* <InLink href="/docs/relationships/1010.html">has time-span associated with manifestation</InLink>
  * <InLink href="/docs/relationships/1235.html">has date of creation of manifestation</InLink>
* <InLink href="/docs/relationships/1257.html">has authorized access point of manifestation</InLink>
</div>

<div className="guid">
Record an element that is applicable and available at the finest level of granularity in a semantic hierarchy, including a sub-type of an element that is marked as mandatory.
</div>

<div className="guid">
All other elements are optional.
</div>
