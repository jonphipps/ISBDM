              Naukowe Uniwersytetu im. Adama Mickiewicza; wolumin)

This example describes a printed volume that embodies a work of non-fiction in Polish
              augmented with illustrations, a bibliography, a summary in English, and indexes.

The manifestation is a sub-unit of a series which is not described.
