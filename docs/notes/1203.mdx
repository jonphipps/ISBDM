# has note on manifestation statement of publication, production, manufacture, or distribution

## Element Reference
<ElementReference frontMatter={frontMatter} />

#### Additional information

<SeeAlso><InLink href="/docs/statements/1030">has manifestation statement of publication, production, manufacture, or distribution</InLink></SeeAlso>

#### Stipulations

<div className="stip">
  Use the element super-type to record a less specific note.
</div>

<div className="stip">
  Apply the stipulations for the element super-type unless indicated otherwise.
  
  <details>
  <summary>Examples</summary>
  
  | Property | Value |
  |:---------|:------|

  *[Full example: <InLink href="/docs/fullex/fx022">Evangelistarium Marci Maruli Spalatensis (1519; Officina Adamae Petri; svezak)</InLink>.]*
  <hr />
  
  | Property | Value |
  |:---------|:------|
  | has note on manifestation statement of publication, production, manufacture, or distribution | "Transliteracija s kurzivne glagoljice na str. 512: “župnik don Pavao Radinić”." |
  
  *[Full example: <InLink href="/docs/fullex/fx037">Mrtvi pisani unutra, Žman (1607-1612; Radinić, Pavao, 1549-1611; svezak)</InLink>.]*
  <hr />
  
  | Property | Value |
  |:---------|:------|
  | has note on manifestation statement of publication, production, manufacture, or distribution | "Publisher changed from K.G. Saur to De Gruyter Saur from volume 36 (2008)." |
  
  *[Full example: <InLink href="/docs/fullex/fx013">IFLA series on bibliographic control (2004-2012; De Gruyter Saur; volume)</InLink>.]*
  <hr />
  
  | Property | Value |
  |:---------|:------|
  | has note on manifestation statement of publication, production, manufacture, or distribution | "Date of publication: December 1815." |

  </details>
</div>

<div className="stip">
  Record a value that gives information about the absence of information that normally appears in a manifestation statement of publication, production, manufacture, or distribution, including names of responsible agents, places, and time-spans.
  
  <details>
  <summary>Examples</summary>
  
  | Property | Value |
  |:---------|:------|
  | has note on manifestation statement of publication, production, manufacture, or distribution | "Date of publication not given." |
  
  *[Full example: <InLink href="/docs/fullex/fx026">Hear the wind sing (2015; Harvill Secker; volume; perfect binding)</InLink>.]*
  <hr />
  
  | Property | Value |
  |:---------|:------|
  | has note on manifestation statement of publication, production, manufacture, or distribution | "Place and date of publication not given." |
  
  *[Full example: <InLink href="/docs/fullex/fx047">Lady Bird (2018; Universal Pictures Home Entertainment; videodisc; BD)</InLink>.]*
  <hr />
  
  | Property | Value |
  |:---------|:------|
  | has note on manifestation statement of publication, production, manufacture, or distribution | "Publication details not given." |
  
  *[Full example: <InLink href="/docs/fullex/fx054">Bridget Riley (2019; National Galleries of Scotland; sheet)</InLink>.]*
  </details>
</div>

<div className="stip">
  Record a value that gives information that is absent from a manifestation statement of publication, production, manufacture, or distribution but appears in external sources of information, including names of responsible agents, places, and time-spans.
</div>

<div className="stip">
  Record a value that gives information about copyright, licence, or privilege that appears in an external source of information.
  
  <details>
  <summary>Examples</summary>
  
  | Property | Value |
  |:---------|:------|
  | has note on manifestation statement of publication, production, manufacture, or distribution | "Licencia de representación José de Cañizares. Dada en Madrid, 7 de junio de 1710." |
  </details>
</div>

<div className="stip">
  If a date does not appear in the Gregorian calendar in a manifestation statement of publication, production, manufacture, or distribution, record a value that gives the equivalent Gregorian calendar date in arabic numerals.
</div>

<div className="stip">
  If a date appears in roman numerals in a manifestation statement of publication, production, manufacture, or distribution, record a value that gives the date in arabic numerals.
</div>

<div className="stip">
  If a date appears as a chronogram in a manifestation statement, record a value that gives an equivalent Gregorian calendar date in arabic numerals and the source and transcription of the chronogram.
</div>
