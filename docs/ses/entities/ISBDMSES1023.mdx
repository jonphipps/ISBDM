<div className="guid">

This is a suggested string encoding scheme for a value of a <InLink href="/attributes/1023">has extent of manifestation</InLink> element.

</div>

<div className="guid">

The string encoding scheme combines values of the element sub-types in the following order:

1. <InLink href="/attributes/1275">has extent of unitary structure</InLink>
2. <InLink href="/attributes/1276">has extent of unit</InLink>
3. <InLink href="/attributes/1278">has extent of aggregated content</InLink>
4. <InLink href="/attributes/1277">has extent of embodied content</InLink>

The ISBDM string template is:

`{has extent of unitary structure}` + "(" + `{has extent of unit}` + "; " + `{has extent of aggregated content}` + " in " + `{has extent of embodied content}` + ")"

The boilerplate string " in " is a short form of " embodied in ".

<details>
  <summary>Examples</summary>

  | Relation | Example |
  |----------|----------|
  | has extent of manifestation | "3 volumes (124 leaves; 150 photographs in 200 pages)" |
  | has extent of manifestation | "3 volumes (150 photographs in 200 pages)" |
  | has extent of manifestation | "3 volumes (200 pages)" |

</details>
</div>

<div className="guid">

For a manifestation that has no sub-units, use the string that is the number "1" followed by a value for the <InLink href="/attributes/1022">has category of carrier</InLink> element as a substitute for a value of <InLink href="/attributes/1275">has extent of unitary structure</InLink>.

<details>
  <summary>Examples</summary>

  | Relation | Example |
  |----------|----------|
  | has extent of manifestation | "1 list (70 x 50 cm; 1 plakat na 70 x 50
                        cm)" |

  *Full example*: <InLink href="/fullex/fx043">Zadar does Banksy (2023; Narodni
                          muzej Zadar; list)</InLink>

</details>
</div>

<div className="guid">

For a manifestation that is a super-unit, a string encoding scheme may combine values of element sub-types for sub-units of the manifestation.

<details>
  <summary>Examples</summary>

  | Relation | Example |
  |----------|----------|
  | has extent of manifestation | "1 volume (122 pages) + 1 audio disc (15
                        performed songs in 49 min 14 sec)" |

  *Full example*: <InLink href="/fullex/fx032">Mojo 357 (2023; H Bauer Publishing
                          Ltd.; volume + audio disc)</InLink>

</details>
</div>

<div className="guid">

For a manifestation that embodies only one expression, use the string that is the number "1" followed by a value for the <InLink href="/attributes/1264">has category of embodied content</InLink> element or a controlled term for a finer level of granularity of the category as a substitute for a value of <InLink href="/attributes/1278">has extent of aggregated content</InLink>.

<details>
  <summary>Examples</summary>

  | Relation | Example |
  |----------|----------|
  | has extent of manifestation | "3 volumes (1 text in 363
                        pages)" |

  *Full example*: <InLink href="/fullex/fx053">Emma (1815; John Murray; volume)</InLink>. The manifestation is a single novel that is embodied in three
                        volumes

  ---

  | Relation | Example |
  |----------|----------|
  | has extent of manifestation | "1 hartë" |

  *Full example*: <InLink href="/fullex/fx073">Corso delli fiumi Drino, e Boiana
                          nella Dalmatia (2011; Biblioteka Kombëtare e Shqipërisë; burim
                        online)</InLink>

</details>
</div>
