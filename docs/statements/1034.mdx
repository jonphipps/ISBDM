# has manifestation statement of identifier and terms of availability

## Element Reference
<ElementReference frontMatter={frontMatter} />

#### Additional information

<div className="guid">
    <p>For a manifestation that embodies the expression of a diachronic work, consider a statement about the frequency and pattern of issues to be a publication statement and not terms of availability</p>
    <SeeAlso><InLink href="/docs/statements/1030">has manifestation statement of publication, production, manufacture, or distribution</InLink></SeeAlso>
</div>

<SeeAlso><InLink href="/docs/relationships/1111">has identifier of manifestation</InLink></SeeAlso>

<SeeAlso><InLink href="/docs/attributes/1238">has terms of availability</InLink></SeeAlso>

<SeeAlso><InLink href="/docs/notes/1202">has note on manifestation statement of identifier and terms of availability</InLink></SeeAlso>

#### Stipulations

<div className="stip">
  Use the element super-type to record a less specific statement.
</div>

<div className="stip">
  Apply the stipulations for the element super-type unless indicated otherwise.
  
  <details>
  <summary>Examples</summary>
  
  | Property | Value |
  |:---------|:------|
  | has manifestation statement of identifier and terms of availability | "Not for resale." |
  
  *[Full example: <InLink href="/docs/fullex/fx034">Stax uncovered! (2023; H Bauer Publishing Ltd; audio disc)</InLink>.]*
  </details>
</div>

<div className="stip">
  Record the identifier as it appears in the manifestation, including embedded spaces and punctuation marks.
  
  <details>
  <summary>Examples</summary>
  
  | Property | Value |
  |:---------|:------|
  | has manifestation statement of identifier and terms of availability | "ISBN 953-222-077-1" |
  
  *[Full example: <InLink href="/docs/fullex/fx019">Hakerska etika i duh informacijskog doba (2002; Naklada Jesenski i Turk; svezak)</InLink>.]*
  <hr />
  
  | Property | Value |
  |:---------|:------|
  | has manifestation statement of identifier and terms of availability | "ISSN 2813-2696" |
  
  *[Full example: <InLink href="/docs/fullex/fx015">myScience Canada (2008-; Scimetrica; online resource)</InLink>.]*
  </details>
</div>

<div className="stip">
  Record a value that includes a qualifier to an identifier.
  
  <details>
  <summary>Examples</summary>
  
  | Property | Value |
  |:---------|:------|
  | has manifestation statement of identifier and terms of availability | "HB ISBN 978-1-4087-0515-5" |
  </details>
</div>

<div className="stip">
  Record a value that includes more than one identifier or terms of availability that are included in a single statement that appears in the manifestation.
  
  <details>
  <summary>Examples</summary>
  
  | Property | Value |
  |:---------|:------|
  | has manifestation statement of identifier and terms of availability | "ISBN 978-1-78330-185-6 (paperback), ISBN 978-1-78330-186-7 (hardback), ISBN 978-1-783-250-5 (e-book)" |
  
  *[Full example: <InLink href="/docs/fullex/fx002">Open licensing for cultural heritage (2017; Facet Publishing; volume; perfect binding)</InLink>.]*
  </details>
</div>

<div className="stip">
  Record a value that includes a statement about the limited availability of items that exemplify the manifestation, including small numbers of items and restrictions on distribution.
  
  <details>
  <summary>Examples</summary>
  
  | Property | Value |
  |:---------|:------|
  | has manifestation statement of identifier and terms of availability | "Il a été tiré 15 exemplaires sur alfa cellunaf numéroté de 1 a 15 dont 5 hors commerce constituant l'édition originale" |
  
  *[Full example: <InLink href="/docs/fullex/fx028">Incendie (1954; Éditions du Seuil; volume; livre broché)</InLink>.]*
  </details>
</div>

<div className="stip">
  For a manifestation that embodies performed music, sounds, spoken word, performed movement, or two-dimensional moving image content, record a value that includes a label name that is associated with the catalogue number.
  
  <details>
  <summary>Examples</summary>
  
  | Property | Value |
  |:---------|:------|
  | has manifestation statement of identifier and terms of availability | "AMMP CD0209" |
  </details>
</div>

<div className="stip">
  If intermediary identifiers can be inferred, record a value that omits intermediary identifiers from a set of sequentially numbered identifiers.
  
  <details>
  <summary>Examples</summary>
  
  | Property | Value |
  |:---------|:------|
  | has manifestation statement of identifier and terms of availability | "ART220BD/1 … 8" |
  
  <hr />
  
  | Property | Value |
  |:---------|:------|
  | has manifestation statement of identifier and terms of availability | "8 disks are numbered sequentially." |
  </details>
</div>
